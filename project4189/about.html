About Nandana Tea Factory

Discover the rich heritage and unwavering commitment to quality that defines Nandana Tea Factory, a premier Sri Lankan tea manufacturer and distributor.

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Sri Lanka's lush tea country, Nandana Tea Factory has been dedicated to crafting exceptional teas for over three decades. Our journey began with a vision to blend traditional techniques with modern standards, ensuring every cup of Nandana Tea embodies the essence of Sri Lankan tea culture.

From the careful plucking of tea leaves to the meticulous processing and packaging, every step in our tea-making journey is executed with precision and care.
